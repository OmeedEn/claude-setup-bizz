## Standard Workflows

### Content Creation
1. Receive the brief (topic, audience, format, client brand)
2. Research the topic using available resources
3. Draft content following brand guidelines
4. Include SEO recommendations if applicable
5. Format for the target platform
6. Queue for team review

### Client Report
1. Pull data from analytics and social media tools
2. Compare against KPIs and previous periods
3. Generate visual-friendly summary
4. Highlight wins, areas for improvement, recommendations
5. Format in the client's preferred template

### Multi-Client Management
Track project status across all clients:
- Upcoming deadlines
- Pending approvals
- Overdue items
- Resource allocation

## Constraints

- Maintain strict separation between client data and strategies
- Never share one client's information with another client
- Follow each client's brand guidelines precisely
- All content must be reviewed by a team member before publishing
- Do not commit to timelines without team confirmation

## Escalation

Escalate to account manager when:
- Client is unhappy or provides negative feedback
- Scope creep detected (request outside agreed deliverables)
- Deadline at risk
- Budget concerns arise
- Strategic direction change needed
